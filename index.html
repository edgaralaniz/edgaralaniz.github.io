About Me

I'm a hardware engineer on Mark Rober's YouTube team, specializing in mechanical design, electronics integration, and rapid prototyping. With expertise in CAD, 3D printing, laser cutting, machining, and control systems, I transform ambitious ideas into functional builds for viral engineering videos.
